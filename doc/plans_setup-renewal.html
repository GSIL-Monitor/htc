Each tier indicates the cost and the duration in months. The price is for the entire period.

Example: If you select a 3 Month tier under SILVER, you are paying for all 3 months. The total points awarded upfront will be 15,000. You camera can utilize these points at any rate during the 63month period.

To the right of each tier pricing is a Cost Per Point amount. The cpp tells you what it costs you per point as your camera uploads media files to the portal. Of course, the lower the cpp then the cheaper it is to operate your camera at that tier. Please make a note of the points cost per upload on this page to better understand your points requirements.

Unused Points are not carried over into future periods.
Apply a Points Reserve in order to ensure your camera continues to operate until you can renew the data plan (no service interruption). The Reserve amount you pay for now converts into points that remain on your account until needed, for example, if you run out of points during a normal period, your camera will continue to operate and use the points reserve. Reserve Points cost you the same as standard plan points. There is no hidden price hike here.

Note: you will need a Points reserve if you plan to use Remote Control which requires SMS commands sent to the camera.
Select to have the system automatically refill your Reserve Points any time they run out. This will keep your camera running without interruption of service. If you use all your points in a given period and as well all your Reserve Points, then as needed the system will charge your credit card on file another $10 and refill your points reserve. Otherwise, your camera will be suspended.
You can change this setting later at any time under My Account Data Plans.
